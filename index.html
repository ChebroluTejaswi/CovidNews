Medical College Information:

        

Medical colleges and the beds availability are given. Some of the general guidelines to be followed at all the quarantine centres are,

1.) Avoid crowd gatherings

2.) Check your body temperature twice a day

3.) Use respiratory etiquettes ( Mask, tissues )

4.) Maintain personal hygiene

5.) Report to nearest health facility if you develop fever, cough or difficulty in breathing

6.) Wash your hands with soap and water frequently.
